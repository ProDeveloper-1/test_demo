Positions

1. Static

The default anf this is the flow of the document.

2. Relative

Position determined by the first STATIC element.
It is followed by the following properties:
 * top
 * bottom
 * left
 * right
 
3. Absolute

Position determined by the first NON-STATIC element.
It is followed by the following properties:

 * top
 * bottom
 * left
 * right
